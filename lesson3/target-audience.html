Target Audience

Those looking for current weather updates in the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas.

Persona 1

Retired Lady: Aged anywhere from 60-70 years old. She is female and has a college degree which she used in the workforce for 30+ years. She is now retired with her husband and they look at the weather updates so they can plan their daily activities around it. They may also be looking at weather updates on their local news channel. They will usually access my website from their home on a tablet or computer at night so they have adequate time to plan for the next day.

Persona 2

Student: About 14 to 24 years old and in highschool or college. Could work part time at a restaurant or a store. Currently attending school and looking for weather updates to anticipate how to dress and what weather to expect throughout the day. They are trying to figure out if they should wear a coat, gloves, hate, sun glasses, rain jacket, ect. They will commonly check the site in the morning on either a phone or a computer.

Scenarios

  1. Do I need to bring a rain jacket for after school practice?
  2. Will it be warm enough for tennis in the morning?
  3. Will it snow while i am driving tomorrow?
  4. What kind of shoes will be appropriate for the weather?
  5. Will it be sunny? Should I bring sunglasses for my walk?
